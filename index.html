In the Shadows

Zendaya Is Making Eye Shadow Cool Again

Soft pastel shades have anchored her looks on the The Odyssey press tour—and beauty fans can't get enough.

By Kara Nesvig July 9, 2026

All products featured on Allure are independently selected by our editors. However, we may receive compensation from retailers and/or from purchases of products through these links.

A decade ago, eye shadow was everywhere—tutorials teaching cut creases and layered, warm-toned smoky lids seemed to be on every feed. But as the 2010s gave way to the 2020s, that maximalism faded into the pared-back "clean girl" era. Now the pendulum looks set to swing back, and Zendaya is one of the stars helping lead the revival.

Though her on-screen goddess look as Athena in her new film The Odyssey is famously minimal, off-screen Zendaya and makeup artist Ernesto Casillas have been playing with light, ethereal shadows throughout the press tour. For the Paris stop they reached for a frosty, icy baby-blue swept across most of her lids and into the inner corners, finished with a champagne shade beneath the brow, a pearly shimmer along the lower lashline, fluttery lashes, and dewy pink cheeks. (The blue came from Prada Beauty's Dimensions Multi-Effect Eyeshadow Palette in 5 Pure.)

It was an earlier look, though, that truly took off online: a bright white shadow concentrated in the inner corners of her eyes, giving her a lit-from-within, goddess-like glow. Since she debuted it at the London premiere, it's been recreated endlessly on TikTok—proof that eye shadow, and inner-corner accents in particular, are due for a comeback.

If your favorite eye shadow palette has been gathering dust, consider this your cue to revisit those cool, pastel shades—an easy way to freshen up your look for summer.
